Who are we?

Spark Lab or the Science of Perception, Attention & Representation in Kids Lab is a part of the Oberlin College research community. We study early childhood development specifically how perception and attention work in tandem with spatial and gross motor skills.

Why is early childhood development so important?

When children hit certain developmental marks you can see their cognitive abilities improve and find any neurodevelopmental issues early
