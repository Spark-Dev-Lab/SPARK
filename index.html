Welcome to SPARK Lab

Welcome to Spark Lab! We give you cool resources and are connecting the community of new parents to the vast world of developmental sciences every day!!

Who are we?

Housed in Oberlin College's Psychology department SPARK Lab or the Science of Perception, Attention & Representation in Kids Lab exists to study child development in kids 0-5 years old. We consist of a diverse group of undergraduate research students passionate about child psychology and development.

What do we do?

We want to learn more about how young children take in experiences and use them to grow learning skills. Not only do we want to further the research community but also the familial community around Ohio.

Why is early childhood development so important?

Early childhood development acts as a scaffolding for how a child sees, interacts and experiences the world as they continue to grow. As they are fueled by experiences and opportunities their brains learn and adapt to certain scenarios and gain the skills necessary to expand their world views. Different contexts and environments can set up a child to react completely differently to one situation versus another
