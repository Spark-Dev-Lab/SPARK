Welcome to SPARK Lab

Welcome to Spark Lab! We give you cool resources and are connecting the community of new parents to the vast world of developmental sciences every day!!

Who are we?

Spark Lab, or the Science of Perception, Attention & Representation in Kids Lab, is a part of the Oberlin College research community. We want to learn more about how young children take in experiences and use them to grow learning skills. Not only do we want to further the research community but also the familial community around Ohio.

Why is early childhood development so important?

When children hit certain developmental marks you can see their cognitive abilities improve and find any neurodevelopmental issues early.
