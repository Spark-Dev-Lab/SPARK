FAQ



Before my visit:


Who can participate?

Our studies are currently focused on children ages 6 months to 5 years old but over time we will continue to grow our age range.



How can I get my child involved in studies?

Through our participation button you can sign up for contact about studies your child is eligible to participate in.



How long does a study last? How many times do I need to come in?

Studies last around 30 minutes to an hour? You only need to come in once



When are appointments scheduled?

Appointments are scheduled when your child reaches the age eligible for one of our studies. When we confirm this info we will reach out to the primary caregiver provided through the communication method you prefer!



How do I get to the lab?

Our Lab is located in Severance Hall, 120 W Lorain St, Oberlin, OH 44074, when you arrive in our provided free parking space a Research Assistant will meet you and your child to guide to our child development lab.

Will I be with my child during the study?

Yes, one caregiver must be with your child during the study, to not only keep your child comfortable but to also in some cases engage in a way that pertains to the study



Can I bring my child's siblings with to an in-person study

Yes! We have a play area/room with provided toys and chairs for other caregivers. One caregiver does need to be in the room with the child participating in the study; however, a babysitter (research assistant) will be present can also keep your child company in situations where you or another caregiver is not present.
