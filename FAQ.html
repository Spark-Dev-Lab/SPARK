Will I be with my child during the study?

Yes, one caregiver must be with your child during the study, to not only keep your child comfortable but to also in some cases engage in a way that pertains to the study


Can I bring all of my children with me if I have more than one child, but only one is participating?

Yes! We have a play area/room with provided toys and chairs for other caregivers. One caregiver does need to be in the room with the child participating in the study; however, the research assistant present can also keep your child company in situations where you or another caregiver is not present.
