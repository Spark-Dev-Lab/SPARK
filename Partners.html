Oberlin Kids

Oberlin kids is a nonprofit organization committed to providing support to the general Oberlin community, through early childhood screenings, events, monthly e-newsletters, parent groups and so much more. They support parents and families of children ages 0-5 and give those families the resources necessary to support their child’s development. The goal is to give each child birth to kindergarten who live in Oberlin the opportunity to be successful in school and in life, to support our children and families, and make our community stronger.
